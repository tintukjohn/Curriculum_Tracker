About

The project is focused on developing a user-friendly interface for both faculty and admin, allowing them to manage and track curriculum requirements efficiently. Using the enlisted documentation information and the trainees' needs, the faculty can plan the curriculum over the course package. By automating the curriculum tracking process, the project aims to streamline the workflow and enable faculty and admin to focus on other essential tasks. Overall, the ICTAK Curriculum Tracker project will provide a much-needed solution for ICTAK, improving the efficiency and effectiveness of the curriculum tracking process by better planning and management.
